ABOUT

Thrift Store is a flea market site for sustainable fashion and lifestyle. Buy Clothing, Footwear, accessories, and Bags for everyone below retail prices. We also resell books of all genres. We have put together regular and premium goods contributions from regular folks and influencers' closets, taking care of everything from collecting the products to delivering them to the buyer. The books sold in good quality. The entire process is hassle-free and the products are in great condition. Our major goal is to normalize thrifting as an art of recreating. To help in balancing a normal lifestyle with a sustainable alternative.
